About Future Scape Hackthon

Future Scape hackthon is an exceptional national-level hackathon hosted by the Department of Data Science at Christ Pune Lavasa Campus, marking the first time the campus will transform into a vibrant center of creativity and technical excellence. This unique event attracts talented participants from across India, all eager to engage in innovative problem-solving and data-driven advancements that address some of today’s most critical challenges. Future Scape is structured as a journey, guiding participants through carefully designed rounds that emphasize data exploration, technical mastery, and strategic thinking. Each round serves as a platform not only to showcase technical expertise but also to inspire new ways of approaching data and product design, fostering a spirit of collaboration, innovation, and analytical depth. Participants leave with enriched perspectives, valuable connections, and fresh ideas to further their data science pursuits. For those driven by curiosity, a passion for technology, or the ambition to impact the future, Future Scape is an unmissable opportunity to contribute to and shape the evolving landscape of data science.

Flow of Events

The Future Scape Hackathon 2024 unfolds across three dynamic rounds, each testing essential skills: problem-solving, data analysis, and entrepreneurial strategy. Participants start by tackling complex challenges, then move on to uncovering insights from data, and finally, develop strategic, real-world proposals. Only those who master these diverse areas will advance, showcasing their well-rounded expertise.

Round 1 : Jiksaw Puzzle
